Second Brain

Privacy & Terms

Effective date: June 15, 2026

The short version

  • No email accounts. The app uses a browser-local guest identity for advanced settings.
  • Share links are persistent and public to anyone with the link. Use them like permanent canvas URLs.
  • Don't put sensitive information in chat. This is an art tool.

What we collect

  • Browser session ID — used to keep your settings attached to this browser.
  • Prompts and conversations — the text you send the agent and the agent's replies.
  • Generated images and canvas metadata — current canvases and persistent share-link targets.
  • Techniques — small Python programs the agent uses to draw or transform images.
  • Basic request logs — timestamps and technical request details for debugging and abuse prevention.

Public vs. private

Public: any persistent share link you create or send to someone else. Anyone with the URL can view and remix that canvas.

Browser-local: advanced settings are tied to this browser's guest identity, not an email account.

Shared library: techniques may be reused by the app so image quality can improve over time.

Your controls

  • Clearing this browser's site data may reset your advanced settings.
  • Persistent share links remain addressable by URL unless the underlying canvas data is removed by the operator.
  • For takedowns or data questions, email secondbrainservice@gmail.com.

Third parties

The agent may send chat messages and prompts to the configured hosted inference provider for model output. Payment processors and email providers are not part of the current no-account website flow.

Terms of use

  • Do not use Second Brain Art to generate illegal, harassing, sexually explicit, deceptive, or platform-abusive content.
  • Your generated canvases remain yours; persistent share links simply let the service host, display, and remix the linked canvas.
  • Techniques the agent writes may be hosted, run, modified, and reused by Second Brain Art.
  • The service is provided as-is, without warranty. Generated images, suggestions, and techniques may be wrong, unstable, or unsuitable for your purpose.

Contact

Questions, takedowns, deletion problems, anything else: secondbrainservice@gmail.com.
